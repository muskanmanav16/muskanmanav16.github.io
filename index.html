Journey

From life sciences to underwriting automation

My career started in the lab, moved into biomedical data science, and eventually crossed into the world of credit risk and underwriting. The common thread is curiosity – using data and systems thinking to understand how things work and how they can work better.

2018 – 2021 · University of Delhi
B.Sc. Microbiology

Learned to think like a scientist: forming hypotheses, designing experiments, and working with messy real-world data in the lab. This is where I first realised I was drawn to patterns and analysis more than pipettes.

2021 – 2024 · University of Bonn
M.Sc. Life Science Informatics

Transitioned into biomedical data science, working on projects like graph-based genetics search, NLP for biomedical literature, and sentiment analysis on drug reviews. I discovered that I love building bridges between domain experts, researchers, and engineering teams.

2022 – 2025 · Atradius
Data Analyst → IT Business Analyst

Started with dashboards, data pipelines, and credit risk models. Over time, I moved closer to the business side, helping define underwriting logic, shaping automation requirements, and guiding developers through the “why” behind our systems.

Today
Technical & business translator

Now I work at the heart of transformation projects, converting requirements into data flows, rules, and logic. My goal is to grow into a technical leader who can design systems end-to-end – from business vision to architecture and delivery.
